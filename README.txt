--------------------------------------------------
HOW TO CUSTOMIZE THIS TEMPLATE
--------------------------------------------------

1. Open index.html

Change the artist/brand name:
- Look for NEON HORIZON
- Replace it with your own artist, business, or brand name

2. Change the hero text

Look for:
FEEL THE SOUND

Replace it with your own headline.

3. Replace the music

Go to the music folder.

Replace:
song.mp3

with your own MP3 file.

Keep the file name as:
song.mp3

OR update the audio source inside index.html.

4. Replace the images

Go to the images folder.

Replace the album images with your own images.

Make sure the file names match what is listed in index.html and style.css.

5. Update the video links

Open script.js.

Replace the demo video links with your own YouTube video links.

6. Update contact info

Open index.html.

Find:
booking@example.com

Replace it with your real booking or business email.

7. Update social links

Scroll near the bottom of index.html.

Replace the # symbols with your real links for:
- YouTube
- Spotify
- Apple Music
- Instagram

8. Publish the site

Upload the files to:
- GitHub Pages
- Netlify
- Vercel
- your own web hosting account
